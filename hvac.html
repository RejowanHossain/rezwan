HVACizel - HVAC Service Web Template

HVAC stands for heating, ventilation and air conditioner. It's a cleaning and household services web template designed for Webflow.

Goal

An HVAC web template that is user-friendly, visually clean, and conversion-focused. It highlights services, trust signals, and strong CTAs for lead generation and ensures easy navigations. It has quick booking options that enhances user engagement.

My Role Research, ideation, UI design

Target Audiences

  • Homeowners seeking HVAC installation, repair, or maintenance services
  • Businesses needing commercial HVAC solutions
  • Property Managers looking for reliable service providers
  • Contractors exploring partnerships or vendor options
  • Eco-conscious individuals interested in energy-efficient HVAC systems

User Personas

Breakdown

  • Simplified navigation: Use clear categories like “Residential Services” and “Commercial Services” with filters for specific tasks (e.g., installation, maintenance)
  • Lead generation: Strong CTAs for “Book a Service,” “Request a Quote,” or “Contact Us” on key pages
  • Trust & transparency: Showcase customer reviews, service guarantees, and transparent pricing

User Flow

One of the primary goal was to create a simple user flow and so that users can escape the learning curve and get their desired outcome with their minimalistic approach.
