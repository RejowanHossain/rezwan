Design & Process

Research

The process begins with understanding client requirements, user needs, and project goals. I conduct analysis and gather insights to establish a clear direction for the design.

Ideation

I create moodboards to explore visual styles, color palettes, typography, and inspiration. This step sets the tone for the design and ensures alignment with the project’s vision.

Wireframing

Low-fidelity wireframes are then developed to map out layouts, user flows, and functionality. This phase focuses on structure and usability without diving into detailed visuals.

Feedback and Iterations

After wireframing, I analyze feedback and optimize the design for ongoing improvement. I also offer technical support to ensure the design continues to meet user and client needs effectively.

High-Fidelity Design

Detailed, polished designs are created, combining the visual direction from moodboards with usability insights from iterations. Every element is designed with precision for consistency and impact.

Design Handoff

I collaborate closely with developers to ensure a seamless transition of designs into functional products. Style guides, assets, and detailed documentation are provided for accuracy.
